# Vijñāna Bhairava Tantra — Sanskrit IAST (Verses 1–5)

This document contains the Sanskrit IAST (International Alphabet of Sanskrit Transliteration) transliteration of verses 1 to 5 of the Vijñāna Bhairava Tantra.

---

## Verse 1

śrī-devy uvāca |
śrutaṃ deva mayā sarvaṃ rudra-yāmala-sambhavam |
trika-bhedam aśeṣeṇa sārāt sāra-vibhāgaśaḥ || 1 ||

## Verse 2

adyāpi na nivṛtto me saṃśayaḥ parameśvara |
kiṃ rūpaṃ tattvato deva śabdarāśi-kalāmayam || 2 ||

## Verse 3

kiṃ vā navātmabhedena bhairave bhairavākṛtau |
triśirobhedabhinnaṃ vā kiṃ vā śakti-trayātmakam || 3 ||

## Verse 4

nādabindu-mayaṃ vāpi kiṃ candrārdha-nirodhikāḥ |
cakrārūḍhamanackaṃ vā kiṃ vā śakti-svarūpakam || 4 ||

## Verse 5

parāparāyāḥ sakalamaparāṃ ca tathā punaḥ |
parāyā yadi tat syāt tattvaṃ tadvirudhyate || 5 ||
